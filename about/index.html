About

Who are you?

I’m an interactive experience designer from the Philadelphia region.

Personality-wise, I’m an INTJ. As such, I value organization, alone time, and logical thinking. I’m grateful to possess organizational skills and a detail-oriented approach as natural strengths.

While leadership is not a natural strength of mine, I’ve been blessed with a number of opportunities in Boy Scouts and college that have helped me learn leadership skills. I continue to strive for growth in becoming a clear, confident, and humble leader.

Why Design?

Art and technology have always interested me, but it wasn’t until I learned about design that I found my true passion.

Many areas of design excite me, but interactive design stands out because it enables me to use technology to execute a design vision, and use design to impact real people.
