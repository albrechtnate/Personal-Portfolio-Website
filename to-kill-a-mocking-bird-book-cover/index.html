To Kill a Mocking Bird Book Cover

Project Brief

Design a book cover for Harper Lee’s To Kill a Mocking Bird to be entered in the Penguin Random House Student Design Award Competition.

Project Solution:

After re-reading the book, taking note of major themes and motifs, and sketching various concepts, I decided on the design above which lets the viewer look over Boo Radley’s shoulder out into the world. A grayscale color scheme was chosen to represent the moral ambiguity present in the book.
