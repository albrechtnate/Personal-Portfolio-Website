25 Hours+ Mobile Application

As part of my User Experience and User Interface Design class I was asked to design and prototype an app. I decided to create an app that would help me and others budget and use their time better.

User research was conducted throughout the entire process, beginning with a survey that gather users’ initial feelings towards the app concept and their current workflow. Paper prototypes were create and tested, followed by higher-fidelity prototypes, eventually resulting in a fully designed prototype of the app.
