Springboard

While interning with Fontspring I was asked to create a web application that could be used internally by company employees to keep track of and organize notes, processes, guides, and any other kind of data. The application also needed to have features that allowed employees to track their time and submit paid time off requests.

After designing the application, I completed all of the front-end and back-end development. This involved learning the Codeigniter framework on the job and becoming more familiar with how to accomplish complex database queries.
