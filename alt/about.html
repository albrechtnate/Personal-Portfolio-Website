About

Who are you?

I’m an interactive experience designer from the Philadelphia region, currently at Cedarville University in Cedarville, Ohio.

Personalitywise, I’m an INTJ. As such, I value organization, alone time, and logical thinking. I’m grateful to possess organizational skills and a detail-oriented approach as natural strengths.

I also tend to be more reflective than vocal, which means I’m careful with what I say, but can also overthink things and lack spotaneity. To improve in this area I’ve sought out several situations that force me to become more forward.

Leadership doesn’t come to me naturally either, as I’d rather be in the background than sharing my vision with others. Fortunately, leadership roles in Boy Scouts and college have helped me learn to how lead well.

My Christian faith is the most imporant factor in who I am. I won’t be prosletyzing or perfect, but I will live in light of the new life I’ve been called to as I pursue my King.

Why Design?

Art and technology have always interested me, but it wasn’t until I learned about design that I found my true passion.

Many areas of design excite me, but interactive design stands out because it enables me to use technology to execute a design vision, and use design to impact real people.
