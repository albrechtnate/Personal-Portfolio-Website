Cedarville Prospective Student Self-Mailer

Project Brief:

Design a mailer that will make prospective applicants to Cedarville University feel valued, and encourage them to apply.

Project Solution

As part of a 5 person group, we designed a self mailer that contained information on the inside spread that would come across as friendly, personal, and encouraging. When the self-mailer was completely unfolded it revealed a poster of Cedarville’s campus that prospective students could hang in their room or locker.

We printed the poster on Curious Metallics Ice Gold paper (not shown) which reiterated the fact that we considered that student special by showing that we would go above and beyond to give them the best.

Our clients, the Cedarville Admissions department and the Cedarville Marketing department, received our proposal well and especially liked how we incorporated specialty paper while keeping the project within budget.
